The Challenge

Business Problem

The Core Question

Amazon operates across multiple global marketplaces with varying currencies, fulfillment methods (FBA vs FBM), and shipping logistics. How can we identify regional growth opportunities, optimize fulfillment strategies, and uncover seasonal patterns to drive data-informed business decisions?

Multi-Region Complexity

Sales data spans Europe, North America, Asia, and more—each with unique currency conversions and tax implications.

Fulfillment Optimization

Comparing FBA vs FBM performance to understand profit margins and delivery reliability.

Trend Identification

Detecting month-over-month growth patterns and seasonal peaks for inventory planning.

Technical Approach

Methodology

Phase 1: Data Cleaning

Microsoft Excel

  • Handled null values in FX rates and phone numbers
  • Standardized date formats (ISO 8601)
  • Created calculated columns: Order Tier, Delivery Performance
  • Flagged outliers and validated data integrity

Phase 2: SQL Analysis

PostgreSQL / SQL Server

  • CTEs for Month-over-Month growth calculations
  • Window functions (RANK, LAG) for trend analysis
  • Complex JOINs for multi-table relationships
  • Aggregate functions for regional performance

Phase 3: Visualization

Tableau Public

  • KPI cards for executive summary metrics
  • Geographic maps for regional revenue
  • Time-series charts for trend visualization
  • Interactive filters for dynamic exploration
Discoveries

Key Insights

70%
FBA Leads Growth

FBA revenue share increased year-over-year, driving over 70% of total sales with stable margins.

137%
Higher FBA Revenue

FBA generates 137% more revenue than FBM, proving it is the primary engine for growth despite having a slightly lower profit margin (17.67% vs 17.72%).

Q4
Partial Data & Lower Volume

Q4 order volume dropped 15.06%, but 2025 data only runs through August—this reflects incomplete reporting, not a full-quarter trend.

#1
Electronics Category

Electronics consistently outperforms other categories across all regions.
